How VECTOR works

Every round starts in Cruise. The multiplier climbs steadily from 1.00x — cash out any time to bank your bet at the current multiplier.

Once per round you can activate Overdrive. It makes the multiplier climb much faster, but it cannot be undone or turned back off — higher reward, higher risk, for the rest of that round.

The round can collapse at any moment. If it does before you cash out, the bet is lost. The collapse point is generated and locked in before the round starts, so nothing you do in-round changes it.

Keyboard: Space places your bet or cashes out, O activates Overdrive.

Demo prototype — no real money. Balances and results are simulated entirely in your browser.
